zigzag image banao

Koi bhi number ya word likho — wo ek high-frequency chevron pattern ke andar chhup jayega. Paas se sirf texture dikhega, aankh sikhudne ya door le jaane par number ubhar aayega.
Kam rakho toh paas se bilkul invisible, zyada karoge toh thoda jaldi dikhne lagega.
Naye tab mein khulega — image ko 1-2 second dabaye rakho (long-press) aur "Save Image" choose karo.
Kaam kaise karta hai: Text ka heavily-blurred grayscale mask nikalte hain, phir zigzag pattern ke black/white pixels ka ratio us mask ke hisaab se bahut halka shift karte hain (kuch % hi) — isliye paas se texture uniform dikhta hai. Blur/distance par aankh fine detail resolve nahi kar pati, sirf average luminance dikhti hai — wahi number reveal karta hai.
